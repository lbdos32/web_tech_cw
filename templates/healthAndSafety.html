Hypothermia Guidance

Signs of hypothermia

  • Shivering
  • Pale, cold skin
  • Blue lips
  • Slow Breathing
  • Slurred Speach
  • Clumsiness/loss of coordination
  • Tiredness or confusion

What to do if someone is hypothermic

  • Let a committee member know
  • Remove any wet clothing and wrap them in dry warm clothes
  • Keep them as dry and warm as possible
  • Cover their head. Use a hat/scarf/blanket/dry towel
  • Keep them awake and talking
  • If possible, move indoors or to shelter
  • Give them a warm drink and something sugary
  • DO NOT: Rub their hands/arms/feet/legs
  • DO NOT: Use hot water/handwarmers to warm them up
